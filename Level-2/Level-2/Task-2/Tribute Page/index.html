Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the "People's President," was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who played a crucial role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam was widely regarded as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile technology. His dedication to education and his inspirational speeches continue to impact people around the world.
